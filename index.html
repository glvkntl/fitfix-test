Princess Leia Galaxy

THE FIRST "NANO EMISSION" STAR RESEARCH STATION

For decades, Galaxy has been the home of science and an example for preservation governance. The Galaxy Foundation signed the return of Earth on the continent with the first ever "nano emission" station: Princess Leia Galaxy.
